What if my co-parent doesn't want to use SafeTalk Parenting Coordination?

SafeTalk Parenting Coordination is designed to work even when one parent is hesitant. Our SMS service helps create neutral, respectful text communications that are less likely to cause conflict. Many co-parents find that the reduced stress and improved outcomes encourage both parties to continue using the platform.

No training required! SafeTalk Parenting Coordination is designed to be intuitive and easy to use. You simply text with our SMS service like you would message a trusted friend. We provide simple setup guides and ongoing support to help you get the most out of the platform.
