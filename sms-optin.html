SafeTalk SMS Opt-in (Via Text)

To sign up for SafeTalk text (SMS) messages, text START to (855) 973-0497.

How it works (double opt-in)

  1. You text START to (855) 973-0497.
  2. You receive a disclosure message via SMS (STOP/HELP, message frequency, and carrier rates). You must reply YES to confirm consent.
  3. After you reply YES, you receive a Welcome message and SafeTalk SMS begins.

Sample SMS messages

Disclosure (after START):

SafeTalk: You're signing up to receive text (SMS) messages from SafeTalk to help manage co-parenting communication. Message frequency varies. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply YES to confirm. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.

Welcome (after YES):

Welcome to SafeTalk! You've confirmed your SMS subscription to receive messages from SafeTalk. You'll receive filtered co-parenting messages and suggested responses. Reply HELP for help, STOP to unsubscribe. Msg&data rates may apply.

STOP confirmation:

You're opted out of SafeTalk SMS. No more messages will be sent. Reply START to re-subscribe.

Disclosures

  • Message frequency: varies based on co-parent communication
  • Carrier charges: standard message & data rates may apply
  • Opt out: reply STOP anytime to unsubscribe
  • Help: reply HELP for assistance

By replying YES after the disclosure message, you provide express consent to receive SafeTalk SMS messages from SafeTalk at the number you used to text START.
